Peakflo Content Portfolio

Strategic content development for modern financial operations platform

Project Background

Peakflo is an automated accounts payable and accounts receivable solution that streamlines financial processes for businesses. While the platform offered powerful automation capabilities, there was a need to educate the market about modern financial operations.

Key Challenge

Building comprehensive educational content that bridges the gap between traditional financial operations and modern automation solutions.

Target Audience

  • • Finance teams and accounting departments
  • • Business owners and CFOs
  • • Scale-ups to enterprises seeking financial automation
  • • Companies looking to optimize AP/AR processes

Content Strategy

Primary Objectives

  • • Establish thought leadership
  • • Drive awareness of automation benefits
  • • Support product adoption
  • • Enable financial transformation

Content Focus

  • • Financial process automation
  • • Cash flow optimization
  • • Best practices in AP/AR
  • • Modern finance operations

Research Methodology

Primary Research Sources

  • • In-depth discussions with financial teams to identify pain points.
  • • Analysis of competitor tools and their positioning.
  • • Reviewing financial process research papers for modern strategies.
  • • Insights from forums like CFO Connect and Reddit Finance.

Content Development Workflow

1. Topic Research & Validation

  • • Identifying content opportunities via keyword research.
  • • Aligning topics with CFO and accounting pain points.
  • • Validating relevance through industry surveys.

2. Content Architecture

  • • Developing templates for long-form guides and blogs.
  • • Creating a style guide for consistent tone.
  • • Structuring content with clear hierarchies for readability.

3. Quality Control Process

  • • Ensuring financial accuracy through team reviews.
  • • Optimizing content for SEO while maintaining depth.
  • • Validating content against real-world use cases.

Technical Complexity

  • • Educating non-technical users about AP/AR automation concepts.
  • • Balancing technical depth with accessibility for finance teams.
  • • Communicating the ROI of adopting modern tools.

Industry Requirements

  • • Ensuring compliance with evolving financial regulations.
  • • Addressing global operational challenges in scaling financial processes.
  • • Navigating region-specific tax and accounting nuances.

Unique Challenges

  • • Establishing trust in automation within conservative industries.
  • • Aligning automation benefits with traditional workflows.
  • • Keeping content evergreen amidst rapid financial tech advancements.
