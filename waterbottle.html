The 2l Water Bottle Paradox

Buying Guide 2 min read

Why did you buy this massive water bottle?

It set you back by ₹300. Sure, you saved ₹500 by getting it from Dmart not Amazon, but that's not what this is about.

This is about what drove you to buy it in the first place.

It was probably the charm of not having to constantly get up to refill a smaller bottle.

I've been living with mine for two years now, using it religiously. Like most purchases, it starts off great. That initial rush of satisfaction, maybe even a touch of guilt-driven motivation to use it more, actually gets you drinking more water. You feel like you've made a smart choice.

But today something clicked. Staring at that last sip in the bottle, I dragged myself to fill it up. As I stood there watching the purifier slowly do its thing, my patience wore thin. "Who am I kidding? I won't need this much water right now," I thought, and walked away with just 1.5 liters in it.

And that's when it hit me - if we still have to spend the same time filling up the bottle, does its size really help us drink more water? Have I just been fooling myself?
